The FacultyHack@Gateway2022 will involve five(5) teams of two(2) Computer Science faculty or one(1) Computer Science and one(1) related discipline area faculty are encouraged to submit applications. Faculty will adapt High-Performance Computing (HPC) tools for use in their courses. They will leave with "ready-to-go" course outlines, supporting data, and identified resources.
Check-ins take place the first 20 minutes of each training session to access team progress and provide suggestions for success. During the check-ins, teams will have 2-3 minutes to give their updates.
